About Paul

More Than Motivation. A Framework for Transformation.

Paul Ochieng is a keynote speaker, author, and confidence coach whose message is built on one core truth: confidence is not something you're born with — it's a skill that can be built, practiced, and grown.

His book ROOTED DEEP gives audiences a practical, 5-part framework for building real confidence, shifting identity, and taking aligned action — even when fear is loudest.
